Our Story

Our love story began as every other great 21st century fairy tale: on Snapchat.


      One lovely morning, Sylwia accidentally messaged Wojciech and, thankfully, he responded. He was a little hesitant but they started talking almost every day as friends. One fateful night, something brought him to ask her one very important question. Possibly the second most important question he's ever asked in his life, “Do you want to get cookies with me?” Which of course, she couldn't deny. After a night of eating Tate’s, drinking Starbucks, and laughing, Sylwia realized that the popular football player was actually a sweet and funny gentleman.

      After 2 years of dating, a trip to Iceland changed their lives forever. Sylwia had the thought that Wojciech might propose during the trip. But after three days and no sign of a ring, she got over the thought and just enjoyed her time. On the fifth day of the trip, they all visited the beautiful waterfall called Gljúfrabúi (sound it out). What Sylwia thought would be just another Icelandic photo opportunity turned out to be her favorite photo to date (thanks Kristina, Piotr, and David). With a smile on her face and debilitating anxiety on his, Wojciech got down on one knee. He clutched the ring for dear life and prayed Sylwia would hear him. "Will you marry me?!" Wojciech yelled over the roar of the waterfall. Although she didn't hear a word he said, she knew perfectly what he meant. Soon the waterfall wasn't coming from the skies above, but rather from Sylwia’s eyes. They went on to celebrate the rest of the day with their friends and overpriced Icelandic champagne.
